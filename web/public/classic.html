What makes it Stillgrid

Difficulty is graded by the solving technique a puzzle actually requires — not generated by a clue count or stopwatch. An "easy" puzzle solves with naked and hidden singles. "Medium" pulls in pairs and pointing logic. "Hard" needs X-Wing. "Diabolical" needs Swordfish or XY-Wing. You always know what you're walking into.

Rules

  • Fill every cell with a digit 1–9.
  • No repeats within a row.
  • No repeats within a column.
  • No repeats within any 3×3 box.

How Stillgrid plays

  • Pencil marks (candidates) on every cell.
  • Undo/redo with full history.
  • Auto-pencil one tap away if you don't want to track candidates by hand.
  • Daily challenge with streak tracking — same puzzle for everyone.
  • Personal bests for each difficulty.
  • No signup. No ads. No microtransactions.
