Common questions

What is classic sudoku?

Classic sudoku is a 9×9 grid puzzle. Fill every cell with a digit from 1 to 9 so that each row, each column, and each of the nine 3×3 boxes contains all nine digits exactly once. The puzzle starts with some cells filled in, and a valid puzzle has exactly one solution.

How is sudoku difficulty decided on Stillgrid?

Stillgrid grades each puzzle by the hardest solving technique it actually requires, not by how many digits are given. Easy solves with naked and hidden singles, Medium needs pairs and pointing, Hard needs X-Wing, and Diabolical needs Swordfish or XY-Wing.

Does Stillgrid show ads?

No. Stillgrid has no ads and no signup is needed to play. Your progress stays on your device.

Is Stillgrid free?

Yes. Every puzzle and variant is free to play, with no signup needed to start a puzzle.
